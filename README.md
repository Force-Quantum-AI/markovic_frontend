# Markovic Alexa Frontend

## Project Overview

- Our client propose to develop a professional, elegant, and easy-to-use web application that combines a powerful AI Legal Research Assistant with a built-in CRM system designed specifically for lawyers in Montenegro.
- The platform will be an all-in-one solution where lawyers can manage their cases efficiently while getting fast, accurate AI-powered insights from a comprehensive local legal database of court decisions and laws.
- The design will feature a clean, light, professional, and minimalist interface that prioritizes simplicity and elegance over complexity.
